Terms & Conditions

Everything you need to know before working with us

What We Offer

  • Custom-designed websites tailored to your business
  • Clear previews before final payment
  • Fast delivery: usually within 1–2 weeks
  • Minor support and updates post-delivery (if agreed)

Payment Policy

  • A deposit of 30–40% is required to begin any project
  • Example: for a $600 AUD website, deposit is ~$200 AUD
  • The remaining balance is paid after preview but before handover
  • We only accept bank transfers. No cash or non-verifiable payment apps

Cancellation Policy

  • If the client cancels after the deposit, the deposit is non-refundable
  • If WebGrant cancels, 100% of any payment received will be refunded
  • Cancellations must be communicated via email

Deadlines & Communication

  • Clients must provide timely feedback and assets (e.g. logos, content)
  • Delays from the client’s side may push back the delivery date
  • We aim to respond to all emails within 24 hours
